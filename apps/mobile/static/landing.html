How it works

Three steps to better chart reading

ChartSignl makes technical analysis accessible to every trader, regardless of experience level.

01

Search any stock

Type a ticker — AAPL, SPY, TSLA — and ChartSignl pulls up a live interactive chart with real-time market data.

02

Run Atlas analysis

Tap Analyze and Atlas calculates confluence zones where multiple technical indicators align. Every level is scored by strength and confidence.

03

Act with confidence

See clear support and resistance directly on your chart, with trading ideas, entry/exit zones, and invalidation levels — everything you need to plan a trade.

Features

Everything a trader needs

Built from the ground up to reduce trading anxiety and surface actionable insights.

Support & resistance levels

Atlas identifies the key price levels where buying and selling pressure has historically concentrated.

Confluence scoring

Levels are ranked by how many technical factors align at that price — giving you the strongest signals first.

Multiple timeframes

Analyze charts on 1-hour, 4-hour, daily, and weekly timeframes to match your trading style.

Technical indicators

EMA overlays, Bollinger Bands, ATR-based volatility zones, and Fibonacci retracements, built in.

Analysis history

Every analysis you run is saved automatically so you can track how levels develop over time.

Calm by design

A clean, distraction-free interface built to reduce the stress that comes with reading complex charts.
